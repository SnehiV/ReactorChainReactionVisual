Reactor Chain Reaction Visualization

This is a visualization of nuclear reactor that illustrates the chain reaction of fission events that results from neutrons colliding with the core. Reactors in operation keep the k Value at 1.

Set the k Value > 1 to see the the chain reaction get out of control or k Value < 1 to see the neutrons die out. You can also increase the speed of the simulation with the slider.

Select those values and press start.

The power level on the display is in Megawatts and represents thermal power of the reactor if there were no safeguards.
